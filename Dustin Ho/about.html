ABOUT OF TRAVEL AND ARTS



ALWAYS BE PROFESSIONAL

STORY AND TEAM

"OTNA" was founded in 2012 by two university friends from Vietnam: Dustin, Katie. Together, they shared a deep conviction in the importance of travel and storytelling for opening our eyes and hearts to the beauty and struggles of other places and other peoples.

"Of Travel and Arts" is a collaborative project with a diverse group of photographers, writers, adventurers, and conservationists. Together we bring readers a world of inspiring and thought-provoking stories.
We are currently a volunteer-run organisation. Over the past four years our design and editorial team have spent hundreds of rewarding hours helping Maptia contributors self-publish some of their most impactful stories in an independent, ad-free environment. The next step for our community is to start funding more of these stories.
So far, stories told by contributors on Maptia have been read over 1,000,000 times by our growing global audience and community.
